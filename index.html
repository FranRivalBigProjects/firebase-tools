Frequently Asked Questions

Why are my Firestore queries slow?

Missing indexes, inefficient data structure, or large collections can impact performance.

What are Firebase security rules?

Rules define access control to your database and storage resources.

What causes cold starts in Firebase?

Cold starts occur when functions are idle and need to initialize before execution.

How can I improve performance?

Optimize queries, use indexes, reduce payload size, and minimize function cold starts.

How do I debug authentication issues?

Check token validation, auth providers, and session handling.
